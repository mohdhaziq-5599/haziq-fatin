Lotus Emira is a British mid-engine sports car that serves as Lotus’s final internal-combustion model, offered with either a supercharged Toyota V6 or a turbocharged Mercedes-AMG four-cylinder, and blends lightweight agility with modern performance.

Nissan Z Performance coupe uses a 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 with around 400 hp, rear-wheel drive and either a 6-speed manual or 9-speed automatic, giving a classic sports-car feel with modern tech.

The Audi RS5 Coupe features a 2.9 L twin-turbo V6 with about 450 hp and quattro all-wheel drive, accelerating from 0–100 km/h in roughly 3.9 seconds with a sport-tuned chassis and performance brakes.

The Mazda RX-8 Type S is a unique rotary-engine sports car from Mazda with a high-revving 1.3 L Renesis twin-rotor engine, known for its balanced handling and four-door coupe layout.

The 2019 Mercedes-AMG C63 range used a 4.0 L twin-turbo V8 making up to about 503 hp in the S model, giving it strong straight-line performance and classic AMG character, though AMG is now shifting to new powertrains.

The Porsche 935 (2019) is a limited-run track-focused car based on the 911 GT2 RS, with a powerful twin-turbo flat-6 and racing bodywork, produced in just 77 units as a tribute to the legendary 935 race cars.
