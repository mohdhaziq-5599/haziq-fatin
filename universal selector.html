Set 1: Margin 0, Padding 0

Paragraph 1: All text is tightly packed. No space between text and borders, and no space between paragraphs.

Paragraph 2: Notice how dense this layout looks when margin and padding are removed.

Paragraph 3: Everything is compact. Removing margin and padding removes spacing completely.

Set 2: Margin 10px, Padding 10px

Paragraph 1: There is comfortable breathing space (10px). Suitable for normal reading.

Paragraph 2: The space between paragraphs comes from the 10px margin.

Paragraph 3: Text is more readable compared to Set 1 due to added spacing.

Set 3: Margin 50px, Padding 50px

Paragraph 1: Very wide spacing (50px). Text appears deeply inside the container.

Paragraph 2: The space between paragraphs is also very large (margin extends outside the element).

Paragraph 3: Everything is spaced out significantly. Useful for visual emphasis or large layouts.
